Our Services

Preschool Program

Our indoor and outdoor programs are well planned to suit the needs individual child, based on their needs, interests and focuses on helping your child grow and develop to be physically active, social, well confident, competent and independent personality by meeting all of the learning outcomes of EYLF.

Our Indoor and Outdoor Programs includes

  • Group activities – it allows your child to participate and work together as a respected team member.
  • Educational play - it allows your child to make their own choice and decisions.
  • Free Play- it allows your child to choose the play or activity of their own interest and develop their own imagination and thinking.
  • Art / craft - allows for your child to challenge their creativity and experiment with a variety of medium where your child will explore their sensory skills.
  • Technology Use – it allows your child to learn the electronic media using technology such as Lap top, computer and iPads.
  • Indoor / outdoor play – it allows your child to explore and participate in different types of environment and play, and learn to behave appropriately in the different types of environment.
  • Performances – it allows your child to build on their confidence to be in front of a group of people whilst learning to follow instructions.

Inclusion Support Program

We welcome child with additional needs such as speaking/communication, physical disability, autism and support them for their development by coordinating with government authorised agency KU Services.

Centrelink Fees Benefits

Hours of Operation

Our service is open from Mondays to Friday, 7am to 6pm and 52 weeks a year. Our service is closed only on public holidays. We are very close to Doonside Train station and Bus stop (5 mins walk).

Car parking

There is parent and staff car park available on the premise. Also plenty car parking is available on our Street

Community Programs

We organise different celebrations and programs such as celebration of cultural festivals and days, Christmas parties for parents and children to strengthen the relation between all staff, children and parents. We also make donations to Westmead Children hospital, cancer foundation, RSPCA cake day, etc.

Sustainability Practices/ Environmental Protection

We teach our children reuse and recycle items that we use in our daily life, develop the sense of social responsibility and caring towards our planet. We have implemented no. of sustainability practices in our services such as use of recycled items for art and craft activities, worm composting, etc.
